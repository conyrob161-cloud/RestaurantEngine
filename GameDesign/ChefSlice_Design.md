# Chef Slice

## Core Gameplay
- Ingredients are launched into the air.
- Player slices them with finger swipes.
- Each sliced ingredient gives points.
- Combo slices give bonus points.
- Missing ingredients costs one life.
- Three lives -> Game Over.

## Recipe System
Complete recipe ingredients before the timer expires for a score bonus.

## MVP
1. Swipe detection
2. Flying ingredients
3. Slicing
4. Score
5. Lives
6. Game Over
7. Recipe bonus
